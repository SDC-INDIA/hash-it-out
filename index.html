About The Event

We are excited to share with you that SDC will be hosting a 12-hour Ofline hackathon as a part of empowering India with Innovation. HASH IT OUT is more than just an event; it's a passion-driven initiative to catalyze change through technology. Founded on the belief that innovation knows no bounds, our hackathon, scheduled for October 6, 2023, at the esteemed Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology in Telangana, seeks to empower India through the limitless potential of technology .

Themes are:

  • Education
  • Agriculture
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • BlockChain
  • Open Innovation

Our mission is simple yet profound: to bring together the brightest minds from colleges across Telangana, providing them with a platform to channel their creativity and technical expertise toward solutions that can reshape our nation. With a dedicated team, a commitment to mentorship, and a focus on fostering meaningful connections.
